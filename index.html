🧪 Design Your Own Unusual Plant!

Use what you learned from the Plant Chat to create a brand-new plant. Mix traits from real plants, or invent something completely new. Think like a botanist — every feature your plant has should help it survive!

1

Name Your Plant

Give it a common name AND a made-up scientific name (e.g. "Crystal Snapper" / Snappus crystalii)

2

What does it look like?

Describe colour, shape, size, texture. Does it disguise itself as something else?

3

Where does it live?

Habitat — desert, tropical rainforest, deep ocean, volcanic rock, frozen tundra…?

4

Special Adaptation / Superpower

Pick traits from real plants below, then describe your plant's unique twist!

Catches insects 🪤 Smells like rotting meat 💀 Looks like a rock 🪨 Rainbow-coloured bark 🌈 Umbrella-shaped canopy ☂️ Lives 1,000+ years 👴 Red sap like blood 🩸 Sticky death-trap leaves ✨ Drowns prey in liquid 🫙 Only 2 leaves ever 🍃 Glows in the dark 🌟 Shoots exploding seeds 💥 Moves towards prey 🏃 Absorbs fog through leaves 🌫️
5

How does it get energy or food?

Sunlight? Insects? Something unusual? How does the habitat affect what it eats?

6

One Amazing Fun Fact

What would make a botanist gasp? Something surprising about your plant!
